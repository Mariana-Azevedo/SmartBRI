The old clock tower at the center of the village had stood for centuries, its hands frozen at exactly 3:15. No one could remember when it had last worked, but the villagers never dared to repair it. Legends whispered that the tower held a secret, a relic from a time long forgotten. Some said it was a portal to another world; others believed it guarded a powerful artifact. On foggy nights, strange sounds would echo from within, like faint whispers or the ticking of invisible gears. Once a year, on the eve of the winter solstice, a faint golden glow would emanate from the tower's windows.
